Suggested

Talkeetna — Sun Jul 12, before check-in

Downtown Talkeetna (self-guided, unbooked)
Richa & Shalin — while Deep & Khushbu are on the glacier landing tour (11:30am–2:30pm), the whole downtown is walkable in about 5 minutes, so there's a solid few hours to fill before the 3:00pm check-in:
• Wander Main Street — historic buildings, art galleries, and shops like Nagley's Store
• Talkeetna Historical Society Museum — local mining/railroad history, plus a scale model of Denali
• Talkeetna Riverfront Park — views of the Susitna River, and Denali itself on a clear day
• Denali Brewing Co.'s downtown Brewpub or the Flying Squirrel Bakery for a bite
• If there's more time to spare, Mahay's Jet Boat tours run half-day river trips from town
Suggested

Seward — Wed Jul 15 afternoon

Seward, after the cruise (self-guided, unbooked)
The cruise is a half-day tour departing 10am, so expect the afternoon and evening open:
• Alaska SeaLife Center — Alaska's only public aquarium, right in town, good with the whole group
• Exit Glacier — a short, mostly flat walk gets you right up to the ice, just outside Seward
• Small Boat Harbor & downtown Seward — waterfront walk, shops, and harbor views
• Dinner around the harbor — Ray's Waterfront or Chinooks both have seafood with a view
Suggested

Anchorage — Thu Jul 16 evening

Anchorage, after Hyatt check-in (self-guided, unbooked)
Arrival is ~1:30pm and check-in isn't until 3pm, so there's a full evening open too:
• 4th Avenue downtown — historic buildings, street art, and shops, walkable from the Hyatt
• Ship Creek — a short walk from downtown, good spot to watch salmon (and anglers) in July
• Town Square Park — small green space downtown, live music some summer evenings
• Dinner downtown — plenty of options right around 4th Avenue
Suggested

Anchorage — Fri Jul 17, last day

Anchorage, before the 6:15pm car return (self-guided, unbooked)
This is most of a full day open, not just a morning — nothing's booked until the car return:
• Anchorage Museum — Alaska art, history, and an Arctic Studies exhibit with the Smithsonian; plan ~1.5–2 hrs
• Flattop Mountain — short hike in Chugach State Park with sweeping views of the city and mountains
• Tony Knowles Coastal Trail — flat, scenic walk/bike path along Cook Inlet, good moose-spotting odds
• Downtown shopping — 4th Avenue galleries, gift shops, and jewelers if the weather's not cooperating

Vegetarian Meal Guide

Sun Jul 12 — Talkeetna

Lunch & dinner:
• Payo's Thai Kitchen — homestyle Thai with a big vegetarian menu, a local favorite
• Mountain High Pizza Pie — Italian-style veg pizza, easy for the whole group
• Denali Brewpub — salads and veg appetizers if you want something lighter

Mon Jul 13 — Denali day

Lunch (near the park): options thin out heading north — worth packing snacks/sandwiches from Talkeetna that morning. If timing allows a stop, Prospectors Historic Pizzeria & Alehouse near the park entrance has vegan cheese for pizza.
Dinner (back in Talkeetna, ~7:30pm): West Rib Pub & Grill (has non-meat options), or repeat Mountain High Pizza Pie/Payo's from Sunday.

Tue Jul 14 — Talkeetna → Seward

Lunch (Girdwood, 12:15–2:00pm, per the day's itinerary): Jack Sprat — genuinely vegetarian/vegan-friendly, right at the base of Mt. Alyeska. Spoonline Bistro is the backup if Jack Sprat's full (also accommodates vegan/gluten-free).
Dinner (Seward, ~6pm arrival): Woody's Thai Kitchen — vegetarian/vegan friendly, near the harbor. Klondike Pizzeria is the Italian-style pick if the group wants pizza instead.

Wed Jul 15 — Seward

Lunch — pack this one: the half-day cruise doesn't include a meal, so bring sandwiches/snacks from the Airbnb or grab something from a Seward café before boarding at 10am.
Dinner: Apollo — Greek/Italian menu, vegetarian-friendly, easy walk from downtown. The Cookery is a solid alternative if Apollo's full.

Thu Jul 16 — Seward → Anchorage

Lunch (on arrival in Anchorage, ~1:30pm): Taste of India or Bombay Deluxe again, or Bombay South for Indian/Nepalese. For Mexican, Mexico In Alaska has a full separate vegetarian menu.
Dinner (Anchorage, evening): Namaste Shangri-La, or Moose's Tooth for veg pizza. For Italian, Little Italy Restaurante or Sorrento's both have solid vegetarian menus.

Fri Jul 17 — Anchorage, last day

Lunch: Middle Way Cafe or Organic Oasis — both vegetarian/vegan-friendly, downtown. Xalos Mexican Grill is another solid pick if the group wants Mexican instead.
Dinner: Likely light or skipped given the 8:04pm flight — grab something quick near the Hyatt before the car return, or eat post-security at ANC.
